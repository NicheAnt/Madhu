The Obliterary Journal:
Lifestock



The Obliterary Journal Vol. 2 Non-Veg is a themed collaborative book containing contributions of several artists and writers.



My contribution, Lifestock, a black-and-white hand-illustrated short story of a dystopic world of mutant hybirds in a society where consumption and citizenship have a morbid twist.



Blaft Publications is a fantastic independent publishing book house, based in Chennai, India. More of their books can be viewed here.
