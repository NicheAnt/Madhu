A twist on the PIES terminology; I suggest a bit of SPINE for everyone.
Social Physical Intellectual Natural Emotional



Educational theories encourage the practice of involving different learning styles and including the different cognitive pathways of learning in and out of the classroom.


PIES - Physical, Intellectual, Emotional and Social


Suggesting that a good balance of these leads to a more holistic individual and collective experience of learning. Layering connections, stimulating responses of multiple intelligences and requiring an individual to express their agency.
