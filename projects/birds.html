Two wings, take flight


Illustrated: Birds commonly seen in Tamil Nadu


Early 2017, working on creating a book on Birds of Tiruvannamalai, with bird enthusiasts, I found their interest contagious. The exhaustive list includes over 170 birds - local and migrant. Wanting to express this new interest, I illustrated a short list to share.

From Top Left:
Ring-neck Parakeet, Indian Roller, Golden Oriole, Pelican, Indian Robin, Flamingo, Painted Stork, Swift, Purple Sunbird, Ibis, Paradise Flycatcher, Bulbul, Emerald Dove, Eagle Owl, Peregrine Falcon, Shikra
