Imagination Patience Creativity Commitment



Imagination was born after her sister Memory.
Though the two grew up together, their journeys would weave in and out over time.They are known to leave cryptic messages and riddles for each other at crossroads, totems and dreamcatchers as signals, imprints of their presence at junctions.
As sisters, they rise from deep and similar place, home. And as sisters, their whole life is an expression of their difference.




Creativity is inherently generous and her heart always tells her to keep moving. She does not need to carry much with her because under her fingers, each object seems to transform.
She often returns to the places she loves. These are places that are intoxicated with her presence long after she leaves. The people of those places are usually busy attempting to be her lover the next time she returns, creating things whispered to them in a dream, sung by their hearts when awake.




Commitment spent years rediscovering forgotten languages. She steadily wrote several books on them after but never marked the date on any. Most people read just one and can never agree with a someone who has read any another. A few people however venture to read more, trying to understand the subtleties of each language.
The books begin and end with a prayer, the full depth of which can only be absorbed when accompanied with a ritual of meaning, inviting blessing from and thanking the spirits.
