A Living Manifesto




·Pay attention to the spirits that guide things. Talk to universal forces.



·Search for reasons older than situations.



·Trust in the infinite wisdom of your body. Care for, listen to, trust.



·Explore without agenda.



·Anchor your learning shrines, your own rituals and your guides.



·Pull Out
As soon as you feel yourself getting plugged into the machine.



·Resistance is fertility.
Resistance is the wild.
Fertility to create.
The wildness to live free.



·Journey with your soul to find the ways you perceive and interpret phenomena.



·Discover and practice forms of expression. Then craft it somewhere you trust with a community you believe in.



·Give yourself times of isolation and retreat to gather. Strengthen and grow. This time can involve molting and healing.



·Learn to recognise lessons in love. Then slowly learn to open your organs to it. (And then the tricky art of undoing knots)



·Understand how an environment affects you and how you impact it.



·Generate.
Channel and carry your energy with awareness.



·Acceptance is the beginning of transformation.
