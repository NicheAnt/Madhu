Intersection with
Gallery Practices



2012 : A set of 5 booklets that are held together in a slipcase. The content for the books was drawn from course material, references and exercises


Book 1: Display

What the language of visual curation can involve to give the status of something as art


Book 2: Art and Audience

Exploring galleries in Bangalore and Delhi, looking at the kind of work, context and response that they have


Book 3: The Gallery

The space, the Art, the Industry


Book 4: Your Gallery

A study into the process of creating a Gallery


Book 5: Our Gallery

As part of the course, we proposed a permanent one and ran a temporary gallery on campus
