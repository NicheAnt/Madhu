Activity Centre:
Game Manual



Made in collaboration with Marpha Foundation, this Game Manual is a book filled with insturctions to play over 40 games. The publication is designed for an Activity-Center in the village equipped with game pieces and board mats. The list is a mix of traditional and contemporary games.
