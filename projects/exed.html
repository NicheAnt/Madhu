Attempting to define the visceral.



A philosophy which embraces transformative learning that emerges from a direct experience which is designed intuitively and intelligently for a particular group/individual, within a safe learning environment and guided by a facilitator.

· ·/• The Fine Art of Facilitation •/· ·


A crafted raw performance set to an ever-changing rhythm, with a participating audience creating waves that wash into the organ of learning.



A fine, fine balance. Good facilitation holds an outcome in sight while planning and designing, yet lets go of any agenda while being present with the group/individual. There is no recipe here that involves heirarchy, judging, rescuing or condemning. And definitely no free/costly dispensing of advice.

There is wonderful science and psychology involved in the choreography of guided experiences in order to make the learning most impactful. Again, remember that there i s n o agenda forced o n any individual. A good facilitator genuinely loves what they do and cares for whom they are working with.-

Build trust and then act with generosity and kindness; asserting firmness when required.
