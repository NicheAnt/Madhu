Work on Walls


A collaborative mural with artist Ishita Dharap for Devrai Artist Village, located in the beautiful hills of Panchgani. The forms on the textured stone walls took life in white, indigo and rust.



Welcomed into the home of the family that began Devrai, Ishita and I had a gift of a week to spend painting. Our schedule looked something like this:


- waking up at 6 in the morning
- making ourselves a cup of black tea to go with a banana and biscuit
- carry the supplies to the wall
- resume painting--- playlist at hand
- till about 10-11 by which time the sun would be quite strong
- breakfast at the house--- winter time had many fresh strawberries, what joy
- mid morning nap
- lunch and long conversations
- nap
- resuming painting---again, playlist at hand, this time turban on
- evening walk/hike/ride with wonderful Rudransh who would show us such lovely insights
- dinner
- nightcap---songs, stories, bonfire


Thank you. Those days were precious and giving.
